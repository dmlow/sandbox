Trying to generate little infographics to demonstrate the five tools of each of 30 mlb teams.

The five tools are speed, fielding, throwing, hitting for contact and hitting for power.

In our case, we use as a proxy for speed a teams stolen base rate (SB/(SB+CS)); fielding percentage as a proxy for fielding ability; ERA as a proxy for throwing; batting average for contact skills; and slugging percentage for power.
